## Key Decisions Made or Assumptions Taken
1. **Design Language**: Created a clean, modern design language with an emphasis on product imagery and clear typography. This decision was based on the e-commerce focus and the need to showcase products effectively.

2. **Navigation Structure**: Implemented a persistent top navigation with categories, search, cart, and account access, complemented by a footer with additional links. This approach provides users with consistent access to key functions while maximizing product display space.

3. **Responsive Approach**: Designed with a mobile-first approach, creating layouts that adapt seamlessly from mobile to desktop. This decision was based on the increasing percentage of e-commerce transactions occurring on mobile devices.

4. **Color Palette**: Selected a neutral base palette with accent colors used sparingly to highlight actions and important information. This approach ensures products remain the visual focus while providing clear visual hierarchy.

5. **Component System**: Designed a modular component system with consistent patterns for cards, buttons, forms, and navigation elements to ensure design consistency and facilitate efficient development.

6. **Assumptions**:
   - Users are familiar with standard e-commerce conventions
   - The majority of users will access the site on smartphones and tablets
   - Product imagery will be high-quality and consistently sized
   - Content will be available in English only for the initial release

## Open Questions or Issues Requiring Maestro's Attention
1. **Product Image Requirements**: The designs assume high-quality, consistently sized product images. The exact requirements for product imagery (dimensions, file formats, etc.) need to be communicated to the client for content preparation.

2. **Content Strategy**: While the designs include placeholders for product descriptions, specifications, and reviews, a more detailed content strategy may be needed to guide content creation and management.

3. **Advanced Search Functionality**: The current design includes basic search functionality. If advanced search or filtering capabilities are required, additional design work will be needed.

4. **Internationalization Considerations**: The current designs are optimized for English content. If multiple languages will be supported, additional design considerations may be necessary, particularly for languages with different reading directions or longer text strings.
